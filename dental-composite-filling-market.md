<p><ul><li>Resin-based Composites</li><li>Poly-acid Modified Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental composite filling market consists of two main types: Resin-based composites and Poly-acid modified composites. Resin-based composites are the most commonly used fillings, made of a mixture of ceramic and plastic. They are highly durable and aesthetically pleasing. Poly-acid modified composites, on the other hand, contain additional ingredients to improve strength and resistance to wear. These composites are preferred for areas of the mouth that undergo high levels of stress. Both types of composites provide effective and long-lasting solutions for dental fillings.</p></p>
