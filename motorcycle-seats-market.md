<p><p>The motorcycle seats market primarily features two key types: bench seats and dual seats. Bench seats, often seen on cruisers, provide a continuous surface allowing multiple riders to sit comfortably side by side. They are ideal for relaxed, long-distance rides. Dual seats, on the other hand, consist of two distinct sections for the rider and passenger, often offering better ergonomics and support. This design is common in sport and touring motorcycles, catering to performance and comfort for both riders.</p></p>
