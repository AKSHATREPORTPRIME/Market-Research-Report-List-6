<p><p>The suture needles market includes various types tailored for specific surgical applications. Round-bodied needles are used for delicate tissues, minimizing trauma. Blunt point needles are designed for suturing tissues like intestines, where they reduce the risk of perforation. Reverse cutting needles feature a unique design that enhances control while suturing tougher tissues. Conventional cutting needles are ideal for densely packed tissues. Spatula needles are employed in ophthalmic procedures, while tapercut needles combine features for efficient suturing in various surgeries.</p></p>
