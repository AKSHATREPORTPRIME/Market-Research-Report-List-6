<p><p>The End Stage Renal Disease (ESRD) treatment market comprises three primary types: Hemodialysis, Peritoneal Dialysis, and Kidney Transplantation. Hemodialysis involves filtering blood through a machine, typically done at a clinic or home. Peritoneal Dialysis utilizes the abdominal lining to filter waste and can be performed at home, offering flexibility. Kidney Transplantation involves surgically replacing a failing kidney with a healthy one from a donor, providing the potential for restored kidney function and improved quality of life.</p></p>
