<p><ul><li>Crab Steering</li><li>Passive Rear Wheel Steering</li><li>Articulated Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The four-wheel steering system market includes three primary types: Crab Steering, Passive Rear Wheel Steering, and Articulated Steering. Crab Steering allows all four wheels to steer simultaneously in the same direction, enhancing maneuverability. Passive Rear Wheel Steering involves the rear wheels steering slightly in the opposite direction at low speeds for improved stability and control. Articulated Steering connects two vehicle sections, allowing for tight turns and better handling in large vehicles. Each type enhances vehicle agility and safety in varying driving conditions.</p></p>
