<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Manioc Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sweet Manioc Powder</li><li>Bitter Manioc Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manioc powder market is primarily categorized into sweet and bitter varieties, each serving distinct culinary and nutritional purposes. Sweet manioc powder is derived from cultivated cassava and is typically used in baking, beverages, and as a thickening agent due to its milder flavor. In contrast, bitter manioc powder, made from wild cassava, contains higher levels of cyanogenic compounds and requires processing to remove toxins. Despite its safety measures, it is often used in traditional dishes and indigenous cuisines, highlighting its cultural significance.</p></p>
