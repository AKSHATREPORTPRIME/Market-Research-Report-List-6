<p><p>The Fire Detection Alarm System market comprises two primary types: Conventional Fire Alarm Systems and Addressable Fire Alarm Systems. Conventional systems utilize a simple circuit design that segments the building into zones, triggering alarms for each zone upon detection of fire. Conversely, Addressable systems offer more advanced features, providing specific location identification for each device, enabling quicker response times. This enhanced communication and monitoring capability makes Addressable systems ideal for larger or more complex environments, improving overall safety and management.</p></p>
