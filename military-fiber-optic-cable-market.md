<p><strong>The Military Fiber Optic Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multimode-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military fiber optic cable market is primarily categorized into two types: single-mode and multimode fiber. Single-mode fibers feature a smaller core diameter, allowing the transmission of light signals over long distances with minimal loss, making them ideal for expansive military communications networks. Conversely, multimode fibers have a larger core, supporting multiple light paths for shorter distances, which is beneficial in tactical situations where data transmission within confined areas is required. Both types ensure secure and reliable communication in military operations.</p></p>
