<p><ul><li>Analog Microphone</li><li>Digital Microphone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive MEMS microphone market is categorized into analog and digital microphones. Analog microphones convert sound waves into electrical signals in an analog format, often requiring additional processing. They are typically simpler and facilitate straightforward integration into existing systems. In contrast, digital microphones convert sound into digital signals directly, offering enhanced sound quality, noise immunity, and easier integration with digital signal processing systems. Both types serve critical roles in applications such as voice recognition, hands-free calling, and advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
