<p><p>The Fluorescence Analysis Cabinets market comprises various types, including Ultra Violet Fluorescence Analysis Cabinets and X-Ray Fluorescence Analysis Cabinets. Ultra Violet Fluorescence Analysis Cabinets utilize UV light to excite fluorescent specimens, primarily for biological and chemical analysis, offering enhanced sensitivity and resolution. In contrast, X-Ray Fluorescence Analysis Cabinets focus on elemental composition and material analysis, using X-ray excitation to detect and quantify elements in solid, liquid, or powdered samples. Both types serve critical roles in research, industrial applications, and quality control.</p></p>
