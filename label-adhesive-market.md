<p><ul><li>Water-based Label Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-based Label Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The label adhesive market is primarily segmented into water-based and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based label adhesives utilize water as the primary solvent, resulting in lower environmental impact, quicker drying times, and easy cleanup. They are ideal for paper labels and applications requiring flexibility. In contrast, solvent-based label adhesives employ organic solvents, offering superior adhesion, durability, and resistance to moisture and temperature variations. These are often used for demanding applications like packaging and industrial labeling, where performance is critical.</p></p>
