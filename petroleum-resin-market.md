<p><ul><li>C5 Petroleum Resin</li><li>C9 Petroleum Resin</li><li>C5/C9 Petroleum Resin</li><li>Modified Petroleum Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The petroleum resin market encompasses various types, including C5, C9, C5/C9, and modified petroleum resins. C5 petroleum resin, derived from C5 fraction, is known for its low molecular weight and is commonly used in adhesives and coatings. C9 petroleum resin, sourced from C9 fractions, offers higher softening points, making it suitable for inks and sealants. C5/C9 petroleum resin combines properties of both types, enhancing versatility. Modified petroleum resins are chemically altered to improve performance, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
