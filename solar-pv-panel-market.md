<p><p>The solar PV panel market comprises three main types: Monocrystalline, Polycrystalline, and Thin-Film. Monocrystalline panels, made from single-crystal silicon, offer high efficiency and durability but are costlier. Polycrystalline panels, crafted from multiple silicon fragments, are more affordable but slightly less efficient. Thin-Film panels, using layers of photovoltaic material, are lightweight, flexible, and cost-effective but have lower efficiency and shorter lifespans. Each type caters to different needs, balancing efficiency, cost, and application suitability in the solar energy market.</p></p>
