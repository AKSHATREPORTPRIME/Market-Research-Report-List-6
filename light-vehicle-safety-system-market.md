<p><p>The Light Vehicle Safety System market is categorized into active and passive safety types. Active safety systems focus on preventing accidents through technologies like anti-lock braking systems, electronic stability control, and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). In contrast, passive safety systems are designed to protect occupants during an accident, incorporating features such as airbags, seat belts, and crumple zones. Together, these systems enhance vehicle safety by reducing both the likelihood of collisions and the severity of injuries when they occur.</p></p>
