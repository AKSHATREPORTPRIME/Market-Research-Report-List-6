<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Shear Wave Transducer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Incidence Sensor</li><li>Radial Incidence Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shear wave transducer market includes two primary types: normal incidence sensors and radial incidence sensors. Normal incidence sensors are designed to detect shear waves that enter at a perpendicular angle, providing accurate measurements for various applications, such as material characterization and flaw detection. In contrast, radial incidence sensors detect shear waves at an angle, allowing for enhanced sensitivity and versatility in complex environments. Both types are essential for non-destructive testing, ensuring effective evaluation of material integrity and performance.</p></p>
