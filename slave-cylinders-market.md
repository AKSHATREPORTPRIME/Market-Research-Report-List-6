<p><ul><li>Concentric Slave Cylinder</li><li>External Slave Cylinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The slave cylinder market is divided into two main types: concentric slave cylinders and external slave cylinders. Concentric slave cylinders are integrated into the transmission system, offering compact design and efficient operation, primarily used in modern vehicles for improved performance and space-saving. In contrast, external slave cylinders are mounted outside the transmission, making them easier to service and replace. Both types are critical for hydraulically actuated clutch systems, ensuring smooth gear shifting and enhanced vehicle control.</p></p>
