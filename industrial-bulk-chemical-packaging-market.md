<p><ul><li>Drums</li><li>Flexitanks</li><li>Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBCs)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial bulk chemical packaging market is segmented into drums, flexitanks, intermediate bulk containers (IBCs), and others. Drums are traditional containers made of metal or plastic with capacities ranging from 55 to 250 gallons. Flexitanks are flexible, collapsible tanks that can hold up to 24,000 liters of liquid chemicals for transportation. IBCs are versatile containers for storing and transporting chemicals in volumes up to 1,000 liters. Other types of bulk chemical packaging include totes, pails, and barrels, depending on the requirements of the chemical industry.</p></p>
