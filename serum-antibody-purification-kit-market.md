<p><p>Serum antibody purification kits are available in various types based on the affinity ligands used for antibody purification. Protein A, Protein G, Protein A/G, and Protein L are commonly used affinity ligands for purifying serum antibodies. Protein A is specific for binding to the Fc region of IgG antibodies, while Protein G can bind to a wider range of antibody classes. Protein A/G combines the properties of both Protein A and Protein G. Protein L is used for purifying antibodies that do not bind to Protein A or Protein G. Other types of affinity ligands may also be used for serum antibody purification kits.</p></p>
