<p><p>The Aerospace Fiber Optic Connectors market is primarily categorized into two types: Single-Mode Connectors and Multi-Mode Connectors. Single-Mode Connectors facilitate the transmission of light through a single path, making them ideal for long-distance communication and high-bandwidth applications, often used in aerospace for critical systems. In contrast, Multi-Mode Connectors support multiple light paths, enabling short-distance data transmission with higher capacity, typically utilized for less demanding communications within aircraft systems. Both types are essential for reliable data transfer in aerospace technology.</p></p>
