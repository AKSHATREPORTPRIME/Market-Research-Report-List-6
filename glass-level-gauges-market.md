<p><strong>The Glass Level Gauges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass Plate/Tube Level Gauges</li><li>Magnetic Float Level Gauges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass level gauges market primarily encompasses two types: glass plate/tube level gauges and magnetic float level gauges. Glass plate/tube level gauges utilize transparent glass to visually indicate fluid levels, offering direct and clear visibility. They are ideal for low-pressure applications. Magnetic float level gauges operate using a buoyant float that moves with the fluid level, providing accurate readings through magnetic signals. This type is suitable for high-pressure and hazardous environments, enhancing safety and reliability in measurement.</p></p>
