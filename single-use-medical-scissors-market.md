<p><p>The single-use medical scissors market features two primary types: Crew Cut and Cusp. Crew Cut scissors are designed with a blunt end and a serrated blade, ideal for cutting through tough materials like bandages and gauze with precision. In contrast, Cusp scissors have a pointed tip that enables more intricate cutting tasks, suitable for delicate procedures. Both types enhance surgical efficiency and reduce the risk of infection by eliminating the need for sterilization between uses, catering to the growing demand for disposable surgical tools.</p></p>
