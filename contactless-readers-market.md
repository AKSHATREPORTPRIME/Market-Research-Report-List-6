<p><p>The contactless readers market comprises two primary types: fixed and mobile contactless readers. Fixed contactless readers are installed at point-of-sale terminals or access control points, enabling quick and secure transactions or entries without physical contact. They are ideal for high-volume environments. Mobile contactless readers, on the other hand, are portable devices that allow businesses to process payments or access control on-the-go. They facilitate transactions in diverse settings, enhancing convenience for customers and flexibility for merchants.</p></p>
