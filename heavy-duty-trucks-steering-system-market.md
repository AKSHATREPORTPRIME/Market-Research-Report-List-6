<p><p>The heavy-duty trucks steering system market includes two main types: Electric Power Steering (EPS) and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS). EPS uses an electric motor to assist with steering, providing a more efficient and precise control over the vehicle. EHPS, on the other hand, combines electric and hydraulic power to assist with steering, offering a balance between efficiency and performance. Both systems are important for improving the handling and maneuverability of heavy-duty trucks, ensuring safer and smoother driving experience.</p></p>
