<p><p>The cannabis extract machine market is composed of various extraction technologies. Sub/Supercritical CO2 extraction machines utilize carbon dioxide at high pressures and temperatures to efficiently extract cannabinoids. Ethanol extraction machines use food-grade ethanol as a solvent to dissolve plant compounds. Hydrocarbon extraction machines employ hydrocarbons like butane for potency and flavor extraction. Solvent-less extraction machines utilize heat and pressure without solvents to produce pure extracts. Other methods may include water extraction or mechanical processes, catering to diverse extraction needs.</p></p>
