<p><p>Bain Maries are classified into two primary market types: wet heat and dry heat. Wet heat Bain Maries utilize water to provide gentle, moist heat, ideal for keeping soups, sauces, and other dishes at safe serving temperatures without drying them out. In contrast, dry heat Bain Maries rely on dry air or indirect heating methods, suitable for items like baked goods or roasted dishes that require a crisper texture. Both types play essential roles in culinary settings, ensuring food safety and quality.</p></p>
