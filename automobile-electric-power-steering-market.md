<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automobile Electric Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Column EPS，C-EPS</li><li>Pinion EPS，P-EPS</li><li>Rack EPS，R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile electric power steering (EPS) market includes various types designed to enhance vehicle handling and fuel efficiency. Column EPS utilizes an electric motor integrated into the steering column, providing direct assistance. C-EPS features a compact design for improved space efficiency. Pinion EPS employs an electric motor on the pinion gear, enhancing responsiveness. P-EPS utilizes a motor on the steering shaft, while Rack EPS integrates the motor into the steering rack for better control. R-EPS combines both steering column and rack for optimal performance.</p></p>
