<p><p>Silicon on Insulator (SOI) technology involves the fabrication of silicon wafers with an insulating layer, typically silicon dioxide, sandwiched between them. This construction enhances the performance of semiconductor devices by reducing parasitic capacitance, improving speed, and lowering power consumption, which is critical for modern electronics. SOI technology is increasingly adopted in various applications, including microprocessors, RF devices, and advanced sensors, making it essential for the growth of cutting-edge electronics.</p><p>The Silicon on Insulator Market is expected to see significant expansion, driven by the increasing demand for high-performance, energy-efficient semiconductor devices.
