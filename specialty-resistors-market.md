<p><p>The specialty resistors market is primarily segmented into SMD (Surface Mount Device) and Through Hole types. SMD resistors are compact components designed for direct mounting on circuit boards, appealing to modern electronic designs that prioritize space and efficiency. In contrast, Through Hole resistors feature leads that pass through the circuit board, offering enhanced durability and ease of handling, making them popular in applications requiring greater power dissipation or repairability. Both types serve distinct needs in electronic manufacturing.</p></p>
