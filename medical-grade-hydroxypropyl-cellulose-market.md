<p><strong>The Medical Grade Hydroxypropyl Cellulose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>L-HPC</li><li>H-HPC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical-grade hydroxypropyl cellulose (HPC) is categorized mainly into two types: L-HPC and H-HPC. L-HPC, or low-substituted hydroxypropyl cellulose, is commonly utilized as a binder and disintegrant in pharmaceutical formulations due to its excellent solubility and film-forming properties. H-HPC, or high-substituted hydroxypropyl cellulose, is employed primarily as a thickening agent and stabilizer in drug delivery systems and viscosifying agent in various applications. Both types are crucial for developing effective and stable pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
