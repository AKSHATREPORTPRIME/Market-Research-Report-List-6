<p><p>The stackable USB connector market includes three main types: USB Type A, Type B, and Type C. USB Type A is the standard rectangular connector used in most devices, primarily for host connections. Type B is often found in printers and similar peripherals, featuring a square shape. USB Type C is the latest, offering a reversible design, higher data transfer rates, and more power delivery capabilities, making it increasingly popular for various applications including smartphones, laptops, and accessories.</p></p>
