<p><strong>The Absolute Encoders ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-turn Encoders</li><li>Multi-turn Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Absolute Encoders ICs market can be classified into two main types: Single-turn Encoders and Multi-turn Encoders. Single-turn Encoders provide a unique output value for each position of the encoder shaft within one revolution, while Multi-turn Encoders can track multiple revolutions of the shaft, providing a complete absolute position. Both types offer precise position feedback without the need for a reference point, making them ideal for applications requiring accurate positioning and control, such as robotics, industrial automation, and machinery.</p></p>
