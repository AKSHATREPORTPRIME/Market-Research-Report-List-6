<p><p>The PET and CT scan market consists primarily of two types of equipment: CT scanners and PET-CT scanners. CT scanners utilize X-ray technology to produce detailed cross-sectional images of the body, aiding in various diagnostic applications. PET-CT scanners combine positron emission tomography (PET) with computed tomography (CT) to provide functional and anatomical imaging, enhancing the accuracy of disease detection. Together, these technologies improve diagnostic capabilities and are increasingly used in oncology, cardiology, and neurology for better patient outcomes.</p></p>
