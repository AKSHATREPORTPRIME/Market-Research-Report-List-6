<p><strong>The Industrial Transceivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-mode</li><li>Multi-mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial transceivers can be categorized into two main types based on the type of optical fiber they support: single-mode and multi-mode. Single-mode transceivers use a smaller core optical fiber, allowing for higher bandwidth and longer transmission distances. They are commonly used in long-distance telecommunications. On the other hand, multi-mode transceivers use a larger core optical fiber, enabling lower cost and easier installation for shorter distances. They are often used in data centers and other short-range applications.</p></p>
