<p><ul><li>Direct Nitridation Method</li><li>Carbothermal Reduction and Nitridation Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aluminum Nitride (AlN) filler market can be categorized based on production methods: the Direct Nitridation Method, and the Carbothermal Reduction and Nitridation Method. The Direct Nitridation Method involves reacting aluminum powder directly with nitrogen gas, resulting in high-purity AlN. In contrast, the Carbothermal Reduction and Nitridation Method combines aluminum oxide with carbon in a nitrogen atmosphere, leading to the formation of AlN while reducing impurities. Each method serves distinct industrial applications, impacting market dynamics and growth potential.</p></p>
