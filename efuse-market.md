<p><ul><li>Latched Type eFuse</li><li>Auto-retry Type eFuse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The eFuse market features two main types: Latched Type eFuse and Auto-retry Type eFuse. Latched Type eFuse is designed to remain off after a fault condition, requiring a manual reset to restore functionality, making it suitable for applications prioritizing safety and integrity. In contrast, Auto-retry Type eFuse automatically resets after a fault is cleared, providing continual operation without manual intervention. These differing functionalities cater to varying needs in power management and protection across electronic systems, enhancing reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
