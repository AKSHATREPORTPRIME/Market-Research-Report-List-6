<p><p>The advanced ceramics market can be categorized into three main types: Oxides, Non-oxides, and Composites. Oxides advanced ceramics, such as alumina and zirconia, are known for their high temperature resistance and electrical insulation properties. Non-oxides, including carbides and nitrides, offer superior strength and hardness, making them suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. Composites advanced ceramics combine different materials to enhance performance characteristics, such as toughness and thermal stability, catering to a wide range of industrial uses.</p></p>
