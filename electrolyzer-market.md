<p><p>The electrolyzer market is primarily divided into two main types: Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzers and Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Electrolyzers. Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzers use a liquid alkaline electrolyte to facilitate the electrolysis process, offering a well-established, cost-effective solution for large-scale hydrogen production. Conversely, PEM Electrolyzers utilize a solid polymer membrane, providing higher efficiency and faster response times, making them suitable for applications with fluctuating power sources or smaller-scale operations. Both technologies are crucial for advancing green hydrogen production.</p></p>
