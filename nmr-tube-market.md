<p><p>The NMR tube market is segmented primarily into two types: quartz and borosilicate glass. Quartz NMR tubes offer superior thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for high-resolution applications and experiments requiring extreme temperatures. In contrast, borosilicate glass tubes are more cost-effective and provide adequate durability for routine analysis. Both materials cater to diverse laboratory needs, with quartz tubes often preferred in specialized settings, while borosilicate glass remains popular for general-purpose usage in various research environments.</p></p>
