<p><p>The Respiratory Protective Equipment (RPE) market comprises two primary types: Air-Purifying Respirators (APR) and Supplied-Air Respirators (SAR). APRs filter airborne contaminants, using mechanisms like chemical filters or particulate filters, making them suitable for environments with lower concentrations of hazardous substances. In contrast, SARs provide a continuous supply of clean air from an external source, ensuring safety in high-risk environments with significant airborne hazards. Both types are essential for worker safety across various industries, including construction, healthcare, and manufacturing.</p></p>
