<p><strong>The Advanced Balloon Catheter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Balloon Catheter</li><li>Cutting and Scoring Balloon Catheter</li><li>Drug-Coated Balloon Catheter</li><li>Stent Graft Balloon Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The advanced balloon catheter market includes several types tailored for specific medical applications. Normal balloon catheters are used for standard dilation procedures. Cutting and scoring balloon catheters feature specialized edges to incise tissue for enhanced lumen expansion. Drug-coated balloon catheters deliver therapeutic agents to the site while dilating, reducing restenosis risk. Stent graft balloon catheters are designed for expanding stent grafts in vascular interventions, ensuring proper placement and optimal functionality in managing arterial blockages or aneurysms.</p></p>
