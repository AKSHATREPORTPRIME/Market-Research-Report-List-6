<p><p>Optical Time-Domain Reflectometers (OTDR) are essential tools used in fiber optic networks to analyze the integrity and performance of optical fibers. They work by sending a pulse of light into the fiber and measuring the time it takes for the light to be reflected back, allowing operators to identify points of loss, faults, and overall cable health. The OTDR market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for high-speed internet and expanded telecommunications infrastructure. With the rise of 5G technology and the Internet of Things (IoT), the need for reliable fiber optic networks is more critical than ever.</p><p>Market growth is further driven by the rising adoption of advanced networking technologies across various sectors, including telecommunications, data centers, and industrial applications.
