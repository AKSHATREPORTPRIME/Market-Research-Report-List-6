<p><p>The nonrebreathing oxygen mask market is categorized into two main types: rack mount and portable. Rack mount nonrebreathing masks are designed for stationary use, often found in medical settings where multiple patients can be treated simultaneously. They typically feature a centralized system connected to oxygen supplies. In contrast, portable nonrebreathing masks are lightweight and easily transportable, designed for emergency situations or home care. These masks facilitate high flow oxygen delivery, ensuring patients receive optimal respiratory support in various environments.</p></p>
