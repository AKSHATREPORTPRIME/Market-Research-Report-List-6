<p><ul><li>Utomatic Transfer Switch</li><li>Static Transfer Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Data center transfer switches and switchgears have two main types: Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS) and Static Transfer Switch (STS). ATS automatically transfers power sources in case of a power outage, ensuring uninterrupted operation. On the other hand, STS enables seamless power transfers between power sources without any interruption. Both types are critical for maintaining continuous power supply in data centers, ensuring that critical operations are not disrupted in the event of power failures.</p></p>
