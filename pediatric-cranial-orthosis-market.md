<p><strong>The Pediatric Cranial Orthosis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Helmets</li><li>Passive Helmets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pediatric Cranial Orthosis market is divided into two main types: active helmets and passive helmets. Active helmets are designed to actively reshape a child’s skull through dynamic pressure distribution and correction forces, often featuring adjustable devices to promote optimal growth. In contrast, passive helmets provide a more static approach, relying on fixed molds to guide cranial development over time without active adjustments. Both types aim to treat conditions like positional plagiocephaly and craniosynostosis in infants and young children.</p></p>
