<p><p>Automotive open die forgings market comprises of three main types: Custom Forging, Captive Forging, and Catalog Forging. Custom forging involves manufacturing unique components based on specific requirements of a particular customer. Captive forging refers to in-house forging operations within a company to meet their own needs. Catalog forging involves producing standardized components that are readily available for purchase by various customers in the market. Each type serves different purposes and caters to different requirements within the automotive industry.</p></p>
