<p><ul><li>Nanopowder</li><li>Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic nanoparticles market includes two primary types: nanopowder and solution. Nanopowder consists of fine, dry particles with magnetic properties, used in applications like data storage, biomedical imaging, and environmental remediation due to their high surface area and reactivity. Magnetic nanoparticle solutions are suspensions of these particles in a liquid medium, ideal for biomedical uses such as drug delivery, hyperthermia treatment, and diagnostics, offering precise control and dispersion in biological systems. Both forms leverage magnetic properties for advanced technological and medical applications.</p></p>
