<p><ul><li>Centralized BMS</li><li>Distributed BMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Battery Information Management System (BMS) market is primarily categorized into centralized and distributed BMS types. Centralized BMS consolidates all battery management functions into a single control unit, offering streamlined monitoring and easier management. In contrast, distributed BMS utilizes multiple control units across different battery modules, enhancing scalability and redundancy. The choice between these systems depends on factors such as the application, system complexity, cost considerations, and performance requirements, impacting industries like electric vehicles and energy storage solutions.</p></p>
