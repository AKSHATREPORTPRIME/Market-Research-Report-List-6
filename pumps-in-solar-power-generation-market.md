<p><p>In the solar power generation market, pumps play a crucial role in the efficient transfer of heat transfer fluids and molten salts. Heat transfer fluid pumps circulate fluids that absorb solar energy, transporting heat to the power generation system. Meanwhile, molten salt pumps are essential for concentrating solar power (CSP) plants, where they move molten salt through thermal energy storage systems, enabling energy release during periods of low sunlight. Both types ensure optimal heat management and enhance overall system efficiency.</p></p>
