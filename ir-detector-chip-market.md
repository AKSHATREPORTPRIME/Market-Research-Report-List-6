<p><p>The IR detector chip market is categorized primarily into cooled and uncooled types. Cooled IR detectors have a thermoelectric or cryogenic cooling mechanism, enhancing sensitivity and enabling detection of faint IR signals, making them ideal for high-performance applications like military and space exploration. In contrast, uncooled IR detectors operate at ambient temperatures, offering lower costs and compact designs, suitable for consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications. Both types play crucial roles in diverse sectors, addressing specific detection needs and performance criteria.</p></p>
