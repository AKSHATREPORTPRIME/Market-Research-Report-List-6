<p><p>The titanium powder market comprises two main types: High Purity Titanium Powder (CPTP) and Alloyed Titanium Powder (ATP). CPTP is characterized by minimal impurities, making it ideal for applications in aerospace and biomedical sectors where high performance is critical. Conversely, ATP consists of titanium mixed with other elements to enhance specific properties, such as strength and corrosion resistance. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, influencing sectors like automotive, energy, and additive manufacturing, driving innovation and performance improvements.</p></p>
