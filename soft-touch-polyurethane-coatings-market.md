They provide a soft, tactile finish suitable for diverse applications. In contrast, solvent-based coatings rely on organic solvents, delivering robust durability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for demanding environments. Each type caters to specific performance requirements and environmental considerations in various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
