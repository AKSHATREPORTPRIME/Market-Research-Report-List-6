<p><p>The wave filters market can be divided into two main types: active filters and passive filters. Active filters utilize electronic components like transistors and operational amplifiers to enhance performance, allowing for signal amplification and better control over frequency response. On the other hand, passive filters rely on passive components like resistors, capacitors, and inductors, and do not amplify signals. Both types serve critical roles in various applications, including telecommunications, audio equipment, and signal processing, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
