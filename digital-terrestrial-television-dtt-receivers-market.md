<p><p>The Digital Terrestrial Television (DTT) receivers market comprises three main types: Pay TV platforms, Free-to-Air (FTA) platforms, and Hybrid platforms. Pay TV platforms offer subscription-based services, providing a wide array of channels and additional features like on-demand content. FTA platforms deliver basic channels without subscription fees, primarily catering to viewers who prefer free content. Hybrid platforms combine both Pay TV and FTA services, allowing users to access a broader range of channels and features through a single receiver.</p></p>
