<p><p>The ISO Industrial Pumps market comprises three primary types: centrifugal pumps, positive displacement pumps, and others. Centrifugal pumps use rotational energy to move fluids, making them ideal for high-flow applications in various industries. Positive displacement pumps operate by trapping a fixed amount of fluid and forcing it through the discharge, suitable for high-viscosity liquids. The "other" category includes specialized pumps like diaphragm, peristaltic, and gear pumps, serving niche applications where specific flow characteristics or conditions are required.</p></p>
