<p><p>The Exercise Oxygen Equipment market encompasses various types used to enhance athletic performance and respiratory therapy. Oxygen concentrators filter and concentrate ambient air, providing a steady oxygen supply for individuals during exercise. Compressed gas systems store oxygen in high-pressure tanks, offering portability and ease of use. Liquid oxygen systems store oxygen in a cryogenic state, allowing for larger quantities to be used over extended periods. Each type serves distinct needs, catering to both fitness enthusiasts and patients requiring supplemental oxygen.</p></p>
