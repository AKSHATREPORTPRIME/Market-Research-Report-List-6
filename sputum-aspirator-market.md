<p><p>The sputum aspirator market is divided into two main types: electric and manual sputum aspirators. Electric sputum aspirators use powered suction mechanisms, providing efficient and consistent performance, making them suitable for hospital or clinical settings. They often feature adjustable suction levels and are user-friendly. In contrast, manual sputum aspirators require physical effort to create suction, making them more portable and cost-effective. They are often used in homecare settings or emergencies, where electrical supply may not be available.</p></p>
