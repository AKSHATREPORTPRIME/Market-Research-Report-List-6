<p><p>The rigid mechanical couplings market comprises several types, including sleeve, flanged, and clamped couplings. Sleeve couplings provide simple alignment and are suitable for connecting shafts of equal diameters. Flanged couplings feature flanges on both ends, allowing for easier assembly and disassembly, making them ideal for high-torque applications. Clamped couplings use a clamping mechanism to secure two shafts together, ensuring a strong connection while allowing for slight misalignment, catering to a range of industrial uses.</p></p>
