<p><p>A stationary oxygen machine, also known as an oxygen concentrator, is a medical device designed to supply supplemental oxygen to patients with respiratory concerns. Unlike portable models, stationary units are larger and intended for home use, providing a continuous flow of oxygen through a wall outlet or while plugged into a power source. The demand for these devices is driven by an increasing prevalence of chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), respiratory ailments, and heightened awareness of respiratory health among aging populations.</p><p>The Stationary Oxygen Machine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.30% during the forecast period.
