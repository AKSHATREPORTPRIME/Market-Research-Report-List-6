<p><ul><li>Non-disconnected</li><li>Disconnected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The driving axle market is categorized into two main types: non-disconnected and disconnected. Non-disconnected axles provide a continuous power transfer to the wheels, enhancing traction and stability, commonly utilized in all-wheel or four-wheel drive vehicles. In contrast, disconnected axles can disengage from power when not needed, improving fuel efficiency and reducing wear in two-wheel drive applications. Each type addresses specific vehicle performance needs, balancing power distribution and energy consumption based on driving conditions.</p></p>
