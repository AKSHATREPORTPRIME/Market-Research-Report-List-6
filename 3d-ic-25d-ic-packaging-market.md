<p><strong>The 3D IC 25D IC Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3D TSV</li><li>2.5D and 3D Wafer-Level Chip-Scale Packaging (WLCSP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D IC packaging market encompasses various types, including 3D TSV, 2.5D, and 3D Wafer-Level Chip-Scale Packaging (WLCSP). 3D TSV technology integrates vertically stacked chips using through-silicon vias for high-density connections and reduced footprint. In contrast, 2.5D utilizes an interposer to connect multiple chips on a single plane, optimizing signal speed and power. WLCSP offers a compact solution by packaging chips directly at the wafer level, enhancing performance while minimizing size, suitable for mobile and IoT applications.</p></p>
