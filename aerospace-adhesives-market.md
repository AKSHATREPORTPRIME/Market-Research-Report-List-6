<p><ul><li>Water-Soluble Adhesive</li><li>Hot-Melt Adhesive</li><li>Solvent Based Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace adhesives market comprises three main types: water-soluble adhesives, hot-melt adhesives, and solvent-based adhesives. Water-soluble adhesives are environmentally friendly and easily cleanable, suitable for lightweight applications. Hot-melt adhesives provide strong bonds and quick curing, ideal for assembling components in various temperature conditions. Solvent-based adhesives offer excellent adhesion and durability, often used for bonding complex materials in harsh environments. Each type plays a critical role in ensuring structural integrity and performance in aerospace manufacturing and maintenance.</p></p>
