<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Regular-Tow Carbon Fiber</li><li>Large-Tow Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon fiber market is primarily divided into two types: regular-tow and large-tow carbon fiber. Regular-tow carbon fiber, typically featuring smaller filament counts, is favored in industries requiring high performance and precision, such as aerospace and automotive, due to its excellent strength-to-weight ratio. Conversely, large-tow carbon fiber consists of thicker bundles, offering cost-efficiency and faster production in applications like wind energy and construction, where weight savings are important but high precision is less critical.</p></p>
