<p><strong>The Variable Air Volume (VAV) Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Duct VAV</li><li>Dual-Duct VAV</li><li>Fan-Powered VAV</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Variable Air Volume (VAV) systems are designed to control airflow in HVAC systems, improving energy efficiency and comfort. Single-Duct VAV uses one duct for supply, adjusting airflow to different zones. Dual-Duct VAV combines two ducts—one for hot and one for cold air—allowing precise temperature control. Fan-Powered VAV systems include a built-in fan to enhance air distribution, especially in larger spaces. Other VAV types may include multi-zone or terminal units, catering to specific applications and preferences within the market.</p></p>
