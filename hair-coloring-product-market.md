<p><p>The hair coloring product market comprises three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dye offers a quick, short-lived solution, lasting only a few washes and ideal for experimenting with colors. Semi-permanent hair dye provides a longer-lasting option, typically fading after several weeks, and is great for enhancing natural shades. Permanent hair dye delivers a long-lasting color transformation, penetrating the hair shaft for enduring results, making it suitable for extensive coverage or dramatic color changes.</p></p>
