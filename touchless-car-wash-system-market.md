<p><p>The touchless car wash system market comprises two main types: touchless in-bay car wash systems and touchless conveyor tunnel systems. Touchless in-bay systems are standalone units where vehicles remain stationary while high-pressure water jets and specialized cleaning agents remove dirt and grime. In contrast, touchless conveyor tunnel systems involve a moving track where cars are pulled through a long tunnel, allowing for a more thorough wash with multiple cleaning stages. Both systems prioritize efficiency and minimize potential damage to vehicle surfaces.</p></p>
