<p><p>The residential fitting market is categorized into metal and plastic pipe fittings. Metal pipe fittings, typically made from materials like copper, brass, or stainless steel, offer durability and resistance to high pressures, making them ideal for water supply and heating systems. In contrast, plastic pipe fittings, composed of materials such as PVC or PEX, are lightweight, resistant to corrosion, and more affordable, often used in drainage and venting applications. Both types play crucial roles in plumbing and home infrastructure.</p></p>
