<p><p>The Non-oriented Cold Rolled Electrical Steel market comprises two main types: fully processed and semi-processed. Fully processed electrical steel undergoes all necessary treatments, such as annealing and coating, to enhance its magnetic properties and is ready for immediate use in various applications, like motors and transformers. Conversely, semi-processed electrical steel is only partially treated, requiring further processing by manufacturers to achieve optimal performance characteristics. Both types are crucial in improving energy efficiency and reducing losses in electrical devices.</p></p>
