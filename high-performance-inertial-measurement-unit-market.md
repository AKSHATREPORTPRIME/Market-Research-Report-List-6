<p><p>The high-performance inertial measurement unit (IMU) market comprises various types, each with unique technologies. Mechanical gyros utilize spinning rotors to detect rotation. Ring laser gyros employ laser beams in a closed loop to measure angular changes with high precision. Fiber optic gyros use light traveling through fiber to achieve sensitive motion detection. MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) are compact sensors that leverage tiny mechanical elements for motion sensing. Other types may include advanced technologies like quantum sensors and solid-state devices.</p></p>
