<p><p>The SAW Duplexer market consists of two primary types: unbalanced and balanced. Unbalanced duplexers are designed for applications requiring a simpler configuration, where signal input and output are not equal in impedance, making them cost-effective for various devices. In contrast, balanced duplexers provide improved signal isolation and reduced noise, ideal for advanced communication systems that demand higher performance. Each type serves specific applications, influencing design choices in telecommunications, broadcasting, and other RF communication technologies.</p></p>
