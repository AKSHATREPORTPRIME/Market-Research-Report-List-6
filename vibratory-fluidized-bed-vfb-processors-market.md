<p><p>Vibratory Fluidized Bed (VFB) processors are specialized equipment used for thermal processing, which includes drying, cooling, and controlling particle handling through vibration. The Vibrating Fluid Bed Dryer utilizes vibrations to enhance heat transfer for efficient moisture removal. The Vibrating Fluid Bed Cooler lowers the temperature of hot particles while maintaining fluidization. The Vibrating Fluid Bed Dryer/Cooler combines both functions, allowing for simultaneous drying and cooling, optimizing process efficiency in various industrial applications such as food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
