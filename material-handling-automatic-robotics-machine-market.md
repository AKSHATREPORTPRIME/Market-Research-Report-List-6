Articulated material handling robots, featuring multiple joints, offer flexibility for complex tasks. SCARA (Selective Compliance Assembly Robot Arm) robots excel in horizontal movements, ideal for assembly and packaging. Parallel material handling robots, characterized by their parallel link structure, provide high-speed and precision in applications like picking and stacking. Each type addresses specific needs, enhancing productivity and reducing labor costs in diverse industries.</p></p>
