<p><p>The asphalt release agent market consists of two primary types: liquid and solid. Liquid release agents are typically oil-based formulations that provide a thin, efficient barrier between asphalt and equipment surfaces, ensuring easy release and cleanup. Solid release agents, often in the form of powders or pellets, are applied in a bulk manner, creating a protective layer on surfaces. Both types aim to reduce asphalt adhesion, minimizing residue buildup and enhancing equipment longevity during road construction and maintenance processes.</p></p>
