<p><p>The aisle truck market includes specialized vehicles designed for efficient warehouse operations. Reach trucks are versatile machines capable of extending their forks to access high storage racks while navigating narrow aisles. Turret trucks combine the functionality of a reach truck with the ability to rotate the forks, optimizing space utilization in tight environments. Order pickers are designed for retrieving items from shelving, allowing operators to raise themselves to pick orders directly and enhance picking accuracy, thus improving order fulfillment efficiency.</p></p>
