<p><p>The antifreeze agent market comprises three main types: organic, inorganic, and hybrid antifreeze agents. Organic antifreeze agents, like ethylene glycol, offer effective temperature regulation and corrosion protection. Inorganic antifreeze agents, such as calcium chloride, provide strong freezing point depression but may present environmental concerns. Hybrid antifreeze agents combine both organic and inorganic components, optimizing performance and reducing toxicity. This classification caters to diverse applications in automotive, industrial, and HVAC sectors, ensuring effective solutions for temperature control and fluid stability.</p></p>
