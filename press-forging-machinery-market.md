<p><strong>The Press Forging Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Die Forging</li><li>Open Die Forging</li><li>Extrusion</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Press Forging Machinery market encompasses various types of forging processes. Closed Die Forging involves shaping metal within a die cavity, providing high precision and excellent surface finish. Open Die Forging allows metal to be shaped freely between two dies, offering flexibility but less precision. Extrusion involves forcing material through a die to create specific cross-sectional profiles. Other methods include ring rolling and swaging, each serving unique applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for optimal material shaping.</p></p>
