<p><p>The Non-Agriculture Smart Irrigation Controller market comprises mainly two types: weather-based controllers and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers utilize real-time weather data to adjust irrigation schedules, optimizing water usage based on precipitation forecasts and temperature changes. In contrast, sensor-based controllers rely on soil moisture and environmental sensors to determine the precise watering needs of plants, ensuring efficient irrigation by preventing over- or under-watering. Both types contribute to sustainable water management in non-agricultural settings like landscaping and commercial facilities.</p></p>
