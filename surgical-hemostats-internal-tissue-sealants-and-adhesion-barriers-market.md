<p><p>Surgical hemostats, internal tissue sealants, and adhesion barriers are vital components in modern surgical procedures. Adhesion barriers prevent the formation of fibrous scar tissue that can develop post-surgery, thereby reducing complications and improving patient outcomes.</p><p>The market for these products is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing surgical procedures globally, advancements in medical technology, and a rising prevalence of chronic diseases requiring surgical intervention.
