<p><p>The Pseudo Satellites market comprises two main types: high-altitude pseudo satellites (HAPS) and ground pseudo satellites. HAPS operate at altitudes between 20 to 50 kilometers and provide advanced connectivity, surveillance, and environmental monitoring, effectively bridging gaps in communication networks. Ground pseudo satellites, on the other hand, utilize terrestrial systems to mimic satellite functions, offering localized services and enhancing data collection. Both types leverage technology to expand coverage, support various applications, and improve overall communication capabilities.</p></p>
