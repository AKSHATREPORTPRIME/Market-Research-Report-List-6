<p><p>Hydraulic diaphragm metering pumps are categorized based on the diaphragm material used, which influences their performance and application suitability. CSM medium pumps utilize chlorosulfonated polyethylene, offering good chemical resistance and durability. FPM medium pumps, made from fluoropolymer materials, excel in high-temperature and aggressive chemical environments. PTFE medium pumps use polytetrafluoroethylene for superior chemical compatibility and low friction. Other market types may include specialized materials for unique applications, addressing specific industry needs in chemical processing, water treatment, and more.</p></p>
