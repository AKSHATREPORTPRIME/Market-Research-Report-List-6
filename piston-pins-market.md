<p><strong>The Piston Pins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-floating</li><li>Fully Floating</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piston pins, crucial components in internal combustion engines, come in various types based on their mounting systems. Semi-floating piston pins are secured at one end, allowing limited movement, while fully floating piston pins are free to move within both the piston and the connecting rod, which enhances engine efficiency and reduces wear. Other market types include fixed pins, which are permanently attached to the piston. Each type serves specific applications, influencing performance, durability, and manufacturing costs in the automotive and machinery sectors.</p></p>
