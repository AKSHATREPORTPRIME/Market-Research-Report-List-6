<p><p>The Front Illuminated (FI) CMOS sensor market is primarily segmented into two types: Passive Pixel Sensor (PPS) CMOS and Active Pixel Sensor (APS) CMOS. PPS CMOS sensors operate with simpler structures and are typically used in low-cost applications but offer lower performance. In contrast, APS CMOS sensors incorporate amplifiers at each pixel, enhancing image quality and low-light performance, making them suitable for high-end applications like smartphones and professional cameras. This differentiation influences market trends and technology adoption.</p></p>
