<p><strong>The Geomembranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bituminous Geomembranes</li><li>Thermoplastic Geomembranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geomembranes market is primarily classified into two types: bituminous geomembranes and thermoplastic geomembranes. Bituminous geomembranes, made from asphalts, provide excellent durability and impermeability, making them suitable for applications like landfill liners and waterproofing. In contrast, thermoplastic geomembranes, such as PVC and HDPE, are versatile, lightweight, and easily weldable, finding use in various sectors including mining and water containment. Both types are essential for environmental protection and management in construction and civil engineering projects.</p></p>
