<p><p>Intumescent passive fire protection coatings are designed to enhance the fire resistance of structural elements. The market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly, have lower VOC emissions, and offer easy application and cleanup. In contrast, solvent-based coatings provide excellent adhesion and durability, often performing better in extreme conditions. Both types expand when exposed to heat, forming a protective char layer that insulates structures, contributing to fire safety regulations and building compliance.</p></p>
