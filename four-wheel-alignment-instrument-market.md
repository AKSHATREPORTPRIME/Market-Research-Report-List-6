<p><p>The four-wheel alignment instrument market includes two primary types: CCD wheel aligners and 3D wheel aligners. CCD wheel aligners use charge-coupled device technology to measure angles and provide accurate alignment data, ideal for traditional setups. In contrast, 3D wheel aligners utilize advanced imaging technology, offering real-time measurements and enhanced precision. They often feature digital displays and user-friendly interfaces. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring vehicle safety, improving tire performance, and enhancing overall drivability in automotive servicing.</p></p>
