<p><p>The Conventional Metal Forming Machine Tools market can be categorized into Bulk Deformation (BD) and Sheet Metal Working (SMW) markets. The Bulk Deformation market involves processes such as forging, extrusion, and rolling, which are used to shape metal into various forms through large-scale deformation. On the other hand, the Sheet Metal Working market includes processes like bending, cutting, and punching, which are used to manipulate thin sheets of metal into desired shapes and sizes. Both markets play crucial roles in the manufacturing industry for producing a wide range of metal products.</p></p>
