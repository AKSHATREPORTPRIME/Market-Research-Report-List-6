<p><p>The steel mill skimmer market is divided into two main types: belt type and disc type. Belt type skimmers are equipped with a continuous loop of steel belt that runs through the oil and grease on the surface of water, removing it efficiently. On the other hand, disc type skimmers utilize rotating discs to skim and collect oil and grease from the water's surface. Both types of skimmers are widely used in steel mills for maintaining clean water surfaces and reducing pollution.</p></p>
