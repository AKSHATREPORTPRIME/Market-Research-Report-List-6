<p><p>Regional oximeter market includes two main types of sensors: reusable and disposable. Reusable sensors are designed for long-term use and can be cleaned and sterilized for multiple uses. They are preferred in clinical settings where continuous monitoring is needed. On the other hand, disposable sensors are used only once and then discarded. They are convenient for single-use applications and reduce the risk of cross-contamination. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in providing accurate and reliable oxygen saturation readings in medical settings.</p></p>
