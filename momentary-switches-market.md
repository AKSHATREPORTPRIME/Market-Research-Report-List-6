<p><strong>The Momentary Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push-to-Break Momentary Switches</li><li>Push-to-Make Momentary Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Momentary switches are devices that complete or break a circuit only while being pressed. The Push-to-Make momentary switch, also known as a normally open switch, activates an electrical circuit when pressed, ideal for applications like doorbell and computer inputs. Conversely, the Push-to-Break momentary switch, or normally closed switch, interrupts the current when pressed, commonly used in emergency stop functions. Both types are crucial in various sectors, including automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics, enhancing control and safety.</p></p>
