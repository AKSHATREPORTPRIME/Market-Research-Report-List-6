<p><p>The medical antibacterial glass market comprises several types designed to inhibit microbial growth. Soluble antimicrobial glass releases antimicrobial agents over time, effectively reducing contamination. Porous antimicrobial glass features a structure that traps bacteria, enhancing its antibacterial effectiveness. Coated antibacterial glass is treated with a protective layer that provides long-lasting antibacterial properties. Lastly, ion diffusion antibacterial glass incorporates ions that migrate to the surface, creating an inhospitable environment for bacteria, thereby ensuring a safer medical environment.</p></p>
