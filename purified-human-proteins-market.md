<p><p>The Purified Human Proteins market includes two main types: Native Purified Human Proteins and Recombinant Purified Human Proteins. Native purified proteins are directly extracted from human sources, retaining their natural functionality and structure, making them valuable for research and therapeutic purposes. In contrast, recombinant purified proteins are produced by genetically engineered organisms, allowing for the expression of specific human proteins in larger quantities. Both types play crucial roles in biopharmaceutical development, diagnostics, and various biomedical applications.</p></p>
