<p><p>The handheld thermal scanners market includes three primary types based on their wavelength capabilities: Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR scanners are effective for detecting moisture and certain materials in low-light conditions. MWIR scanners excel at identifying temperature differences and are commonly used in military and industrial applications. LWIR scanners specialize in thermal imaging, making them ideal for applications like HVAC monitoring and surveillance, as they can detect heat emitted by objects.</p></p>
