<p><ul><li>Steel sucker rod</li><li>FRP sucker rod</li><li>Hollow sucker rod</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sucker rod market features three primary types: steel sucker rods, FRP (fiber-reinforced plastic) sucker rods, and hollow sucker rods. Steel sucker rods are widely used for their strength and durability, suitable for conventional pumping applications. FRP sucker rods offer corrosion resistance and reduced weight, making them ideal for harsh environments and offshore drilling. Hollow sucker rods are designed for enhanced fluid flow and can reduce weight while maintaining structural integrity, providing efficiency in various oil extraction scenarios.</p></p>
