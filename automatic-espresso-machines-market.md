<p><strong>The Automatic Espresso Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-automatic Espresso Machine</li><li>Fully Automatic Espresso Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic espresso machines market is primarily divided into semi-automatic and fully automatic segments. Semi-automatic machines require user intervention for grinding and tamping the coffee but automate the brewing process, offering control over the shot. Conversely, fully automatic machines handle grinding, tamping, and brewing with minimal user input, providing convenience and consistency. Both types cater to a range of consumers, from coffee enthusiasts seeking craft in brewing to those desiring a quick, hassle-free coffee experience.</p></p>
