<p><p>The Advanced Lead-Acid Batteries market encompasses various types, primarily including VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid) batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed and maintenance-free, offering higher efficiency and lower self-discharge rates, making them suitable for applications like backup power systems. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, require regular maintenance and are often used in industrial applications due to their robust performance and longevity. Other battery types may include sealed lead-acid variations and specialty designs, catering to diverse energy storage needs.</p></p>
