<p><p>The Hydraulic Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) market is segmented into various types based on channel and sensor configurations. Four-channel, four-sensor systems offer individual wheel control for enhanced stability in vehicles. Three-channel, three-sensor setups are common in light trucks, managing front wheels separately while linking rear wheels. One-channel, one-sensor systems are simpler, often used in older vehicles, providing basic anti-lock functionality. These variations cater to different vehicle types, performance requirements, and cost considerations, influencing consumer preferences and market trends.</p></p>
