<p><p>The meat glue market is categorized into three types: animal-based, plant-based, and microbial. Plant-based options utilize proteins from sources like legumes and can offer a vegetarian alternative. Microbial meat glue, derived from enzymes produced by microorganisms, is gaining popularity for its efficiency and wide application in various food products. Each type serves to enhance texture and binding in meat production.</p></p>
