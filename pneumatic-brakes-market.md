<p><strong>The Pneumatic Brakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaft Pneumatic Brakes</li><li>Disc Pneumatic Brakes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic brakes market is primarily segmented into two types: shaft pneumatic brakes and disc pneumatic brakes. Shaft pneumatic brakes utilize compressed air to apply force on a rotating shaft, providing effective stopping power in various industrial applications. In contrast, disc pneumatic brakes function by exerting pressure on a rotating disc, allowing for rapid deceleration and controlled braking with minimal wear. Both types are essential in industries like manufacturing and automotive, enhancing safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
