<p><p>The brass valves market comprises various types, including gate, globe, ball, and check valves, along with other specialty valves. Gate valves are used for on/off control with minimal pressure drop. Globe valves provide precise flow regulation. Ball valves offer quick shut-off and durability, making them ideal for various applications. Check valves prevent backflow, ensuring fluid flows in one direction. The "others" category includes specialized valves catering to unique industrial needs, contributing to the overall versatility of the brass valves market.</p></p>
