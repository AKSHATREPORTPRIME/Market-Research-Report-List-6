<p><p>The polyethylene masterbatches market is segmented into three main types: LDPE, HDPE, and LLDPE masterbatches. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) masterbatches are known for flexibility and clarity, often used in packaging applications. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) masterbatches offer strength and resistance, making them suitable for containers and industrial applications. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) masterbatches combine the characteristics of both LDPE and HDPE, providing enhanced elasticity and puncture resistance, ideal for films and flexible packaging solutions.</p></p>
