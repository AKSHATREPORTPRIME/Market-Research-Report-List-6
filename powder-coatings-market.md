<p><ul><li>Thermoset Powder Coating</li><li>Thermoplastic Powder Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powder coatings market is divided into two main types: thermoset and thermoplastic powder coatings. Thermoset powder coatings, which undergo a curing process when heated, offer excellent durability and resistance to chemicals and heat, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, thermoplastic powder coatings melt and flow when heated but do not chemically change, allowing for easy rework and recycling. These coatings are commonly used in diverse industries, providing aesthetic options alongside protective features.</p></p>
