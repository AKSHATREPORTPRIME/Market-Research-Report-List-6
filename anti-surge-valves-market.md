<p><p>The anti-surge valves market encompasses three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric anti-surge valves. Pneumatic anti-surge valves utilize compressed air for actuation, offering rapid response and reliability in varying conditions. Hydraulic anti-surge valves employ fluid pressure to control flow, suitable for high-power applications. Electric anti-surge valves use electric actuators, providing precise control and integration with automation systems. Each type is essential in managing pressure fluctuations and protecting equipment in various industrial applications, ensuring operational safety and efficiency.</p></p>
