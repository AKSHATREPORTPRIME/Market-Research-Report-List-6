<p><ul><li>Analog Video Cable</li><li>Digital Video Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video cables market features two primary types: analog video cables and digital video cables. Analog video cables transmit signals in a continuous waveform, commonly used for older devices like VHS players, providing varying quality based on distance. In contrast, digital video cables carry data as discrete signals, ensuring higher fidelity and compatibility with modern devices like Blu-ray players and high-definition televisions. Digital cables include HDMI and DisplayPort, offering superior resolution and sound quality, making them the preferred choice in today’s technology-driven environment.</p></p>
