<p><p>The PEX-EVOH pipe market includes various types such as PEX-A, PEX-B, PEX-C, and PEX-AL-PEX pipes. PEX-B pipes, manufactured through a different process, are known for their cost-effectiveness and dimensional stability. PEX-C pipes are cross-linked via radiation, providing excellent thermal stability. PEX-AL-PEX pipes combine PEX with an aluminum layer, enhancing durability and reducing oxygen permeation, suitable for heating and plumbing applications.</p></p>
