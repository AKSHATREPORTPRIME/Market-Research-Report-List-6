<p><p>Differential Pressure Sensor ICs are available in two main market types: Surface Mount and Through Hole. Surface Mount ICs are designed to be mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, offering a smaller footprint and easier assembly. Through Hole ICs, on the other hand, have leads that require insertion through holes in the PCB for soldering, providing a more robust mechanical connection. Both types cater to different assembly preferences and requirements in various industries such as automotive, HVAC, and medical devices.</p></p>
