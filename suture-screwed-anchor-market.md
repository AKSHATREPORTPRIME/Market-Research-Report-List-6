<p><ul><li>Absorbable</li><li>Non-Absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The suture screw anchor market is categorized into two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable anchors. Absorbable anchors are designed to degrade over time within the body, eliminating the need for surgical removal and reducing long-term complications. They are often used in less demanding applications where temporary support is sufficient. In contrast, non-absorbable anchors are made of durable materials that remain in the body permanently, providing lasting support for more significant repairs in orthopedic and soft tissue applications.</p></p>
