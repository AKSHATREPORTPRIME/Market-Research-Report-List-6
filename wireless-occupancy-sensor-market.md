<p><strong>The Wireless Occupancy Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Infrared</li><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Dual Technology (PIR and Ultrasonic)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless occupancy sensors can be categorized into several types based on their technology. Passive Infrared (PIR) sensors detect changes in infrared radiation from moving objects, typically humans. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to detect motion, providing a wider coverage area. Dual Technology sensors combine both PIR and ultrasonic technologies to reduce false triggers and enhance accuracy. Additionally, there are other types that may utilize technologies like microwave sensing or advanced optical systems, catering to diverse application needs in occupancy detection.</p></p>
