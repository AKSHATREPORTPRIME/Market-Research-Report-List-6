<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Isocyanate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based Blocked Isocyanate</li><li>Solvent-based Blocked Isocyanate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Isocyanate market primarily comprises two types: water-based blocked isocyanate and solvent-based blocked isocyanate. Water-based blocked isocyanates are eco-friendly options, utilizing water as a carrier, which reduces volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and environmental impact. In contrast, solvent-based blocked isocyanates employ organic solvents for application, offering high performance and versatility but with increased VOC emissions. Both types are essential in automotive, construction, and furniture industries, providing durable coatings and adhesives tailored to specific environmental and performance requirements.</p></p>
