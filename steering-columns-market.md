<p><p>The steering columns market encompasses three main types: non-adjustable, manually adjustable, and electrically adjustable steering columns. Manually adjustable columns provide users the flexibility to change the steering height and angle with a physical lever. Electrically adjustable steering columns enhance convenience, allowing for effortless adjustments through electronic controls, often integrated with memory functions for personalized settings. Each type caters to different consumer demands for functionality and comfort in vehicles.</p></p>
