<p><ul><li>Primary Antibody</li><li>Secondary Antibody</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biotinylated polyclonal antibody market is categorized into primary and secondary antibodies. Primary antibodies are designed to specifically bind to target antigens in various biological assays, offering high specificity and sensitivity. Secondary antibodies, labeled with biotin, are used to detect and amplify the signal from primary antibodies. This dual system enhances the visualization of the antigen-antibody complex, making them essential in research, diagnostics, and therapeutic applications. Both types contribute significantly to the advancement of immunological studies.</p></p>
