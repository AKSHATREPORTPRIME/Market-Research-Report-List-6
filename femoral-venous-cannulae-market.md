<p><strong>The Femoral Venous Cannulae Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Two-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The femoral venous cannulae market is divided into two types: single-stage and two-stage. Single-stage cannulae allow for immediate access to the femoral vein, facilitating rapid insertion and use in emergencies. In contrast, two-stage cannulae consist of an initial insertion followed by a second stage where the catheter is placed more securely, often enhancing stability and reducing complications. Both types cater to different clinical needs, influencing their usage in critical care and surgical settings.</p></p>
