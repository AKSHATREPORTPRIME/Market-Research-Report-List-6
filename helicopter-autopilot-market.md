Attitude Hold maintains a helicopter's orientation, ensuring consistent flight. Coupled systems guide the helicopter along predefined flight paths, seamlessly integrating with navigation systems. SAS enhances stability during flight, correcting for disturbances and improving control. The "Others" category encompasses additional advanced autopilot systems and emerging technologies, catering to diverse operational requirements and enhancing overall flight safety and efficiency.</p></p>
