<p><p>Automotive telematics systems can be categorized into three main types: embedded, tethered, and integrated. </p><p>Embedded type systems are built directly into the vehicle, offering complete functionality without relying on external devices. Tethered type systems use a smartphone or another mobile device to connect to the vehicle, providing access to telematics features through a companion app. Integrated type systems combine both embedded capabilities with cloud-based services, allowing real-time data sharing and enhanced user experience, facilitating a seamless connection between the vehicle and external networks.</p></p>
