<p><strong>The Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uncooled Infrared Detector</li><li>Cooled Infrared Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared detector market primarily consists of two main types: uncooled and cooled infrared detectors. Uncooled infrared detectors operate at ambient temperatures, making them more compact, cost-effective, and easier to integrate into various applications like automotive and consumer electronics. Cooled infrared detectors, on the other hand, require refrigeration to achieve high sensitivity and are typically used in military, aerospace, and advanced surveillance systems. The choice between the two types depends on application requirements, sensitivity needs, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
