Single acting pins engage in one direction, providing straightforward locking mechanisms for easy operation. Double acting pins offer locking solutions in both directions, enhancing versatility and security in applications. Detent pins feature a spring-loaded mechanism that securely holds parts in place while allowing for quick release, making them ideal for applications requiring frequent adjustments or removals in aviation settings.</p></p>
