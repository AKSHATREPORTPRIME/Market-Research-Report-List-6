<p><strong>The Platinum RTD Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-Wound RTD</li><li>Thin Film RTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Platinum RTD sensors market includes two primary types: wire-wound RTD sensors and thin film RTD sensors. Wire-wound RTDs consist of a thin wire wound around a ceramic or glass core, allowing for high accuracy and stability in temperature measurement. In contrast, thin film RTDs are created by depositing a thin film of platinum onto a substrate, offering advantages such as smaller size and faster response times. Both types are critical in various industrial and laboratory applications for precise temperature sensing.</p></p>
