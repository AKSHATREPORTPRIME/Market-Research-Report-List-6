<p><p>The automotive glass market for passenger cars includes three main types: tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass, known for its strength and safety, shatters into small, blunt pieces upon impact, reducing injury risks. Laminated glass consists of two layers with a safety interlayer, preventing shattering and enhancing sound insulation. Other types may include specialty glasses, such as acoustic or solar control glass, designed for specific performance or aesthetic features. Each type contributes to vehicle safety, comfort, and functionality.</p></p>
