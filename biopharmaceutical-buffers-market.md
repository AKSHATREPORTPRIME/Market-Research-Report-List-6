<p><p>The biopharmaceutical buffers market is segmented into sterile and non-sterile buffers. Sterile buffers are designed for use in injectable formulations, ensuring safety and preventing contamination during manufacturing and storage. They are crucial for maintaining the stability and efficacy of biopharmaceutical products. In contrast, non-sterile buffers are utilized in processes where sterility is not a critical factor, such as during the production of bulk intermediates. Both types play essential roles in biopharmaceutical development and quality control.</p></p>
