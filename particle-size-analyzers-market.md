<p><p>Particle-size analyzers are crucial in determining the size distribution of particles in various industries. Laser diffraction measures size based on scattered light patterns. Dynamic light scattering (DLS) analyzes particle motion in a fluid to estimate size. Image analysis uses microscopy and software to evaluate particle shapes and sizes. The Coulter Principle detects changes in electrical resistance as particles pass through an aperture. Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis visualizes and tracks individual particles in a fluid. Other methods include sedimentation and sieve analysis.</p></p>
