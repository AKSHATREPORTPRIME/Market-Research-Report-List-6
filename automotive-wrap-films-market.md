<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Wrap Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Film</li><li>Calendered Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wrap films market primarily consists of two types: cast films and calendered films. Cast films are produced by melting and casting polymer onto a surface, resulting in smoother finishes and better durability, making them ideal for high-quality graphics and intricate designs. In contrast, calendered films are made by compressing plastic between rollers, offering a thicker, more rigid structure. They are generally more cost-effective and suitable for simpler applications, yet may lack the flexibility and longevity of cast films.</p></p>
