<p><p>Machine vision cameras are essential for industrial applications, and they primarily fall into two categories: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture images in a two-dimensional format, providing a complete view of the scene in a single shot, making them ideal for applications like Quality Control and Inspection. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, suitable for continuous applications such as web inspection or material handling, where length and detail are critical.</p></p>
