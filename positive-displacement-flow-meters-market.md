<p><p>The Positive Displacement Flow Meters market is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I meters, often used in custody transfer applications, provide high accuracy and reliability for measuring liquids. They utilize mechanisms like gears or diaphragm chambers to displace a fixed volume of fluid. Type II meters typically focus on lower flow rates and less demanding applications, employing simpler mechanisms for cost-effectiveness, yet still ensuring reasonable accuracy for various industrial uses.</p></p>
