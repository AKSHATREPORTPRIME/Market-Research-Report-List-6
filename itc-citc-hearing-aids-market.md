CIC aids are even smaller, fitting entirely within the ear canal for enhanced discreteness and comfort. Both types offer tailored sound amplification while mitigating feedback, making them suitable for mild to moderate hearing losses, appealing to users seeking discreet and effective solutions.</p></p>
