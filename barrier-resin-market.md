PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride) offers excellent resistance to moisture and oxygen, enhancing shelf life. Other barrier resins may include polyamide and PET, each serving specific applications across industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods, ensuring product integrity and longevity.</p></p>
