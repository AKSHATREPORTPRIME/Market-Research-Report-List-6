<p><p>The Thermal Management Products for Semiconductor Microchips market can be classified into two primary types: passive thermal management products and active thermal management products. Passive thermal management products include heat sinks, heat spreaders, and thermal interface materials, which are designed to dissipate heat passively without the need for external power or moving parts. Active thermal management products, on the other hand, include cooling fans, heat pumps, and liquid cooling systems, which actively remove heat from semiconductor microchips using energy sources such as electricity or mechanical motion.</p></p>
