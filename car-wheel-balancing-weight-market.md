<p><ul><li>Clip-On Type</li><li>Adhesive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car wheel balancing weight market primarily consists of two types: clip-on and adhesive weights. Clip-on weights are attached to the rim of the wheel using a metal clip, providing easy installation and removal for adjustments. Adhesive weights, on the other hand, are mounted directly on the wheel using strong adhesive, offering a cleaner appearance and preventing potential damage to the rim. Both types are designed to ensure proper wheel balance, improving vehicle performance and enhancing tire life.</p></p>
