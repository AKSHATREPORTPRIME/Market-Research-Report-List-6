<p><p>The reflective box liner market comprises various types focused on packaging solutions, primarily categorized into void fill and cushioning materials. Void fill is designed to occupy empty spaces within packages, preventing movement and damage during transit. Cushioning materials, on the other hand, provide shock absorption and protection for fragile items, ensuring they remain safe from impacts. Both types enhance the protective qualities of packaging while improving thermal insulation, making them suitable for temperature-sensitive products.</p></p>
