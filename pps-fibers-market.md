<p><ul><li>PPS Filaments</li><li>PPS Staple Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PPS fibers market comprises two primary types: PPS filaments and PPS staple fibers. PPS filaments are continuous strands used in applications requiring high strength and durability, such as textiles and automotive components. In contrast, PPS staple fibers are cut lengths of PPS fibers used in non-woven fabrics, insulation materials, and composite manufacturing. Both types leverage PPS's excellent thermal resistance, chemical stability, and mechanical properties, making them suitable for demanding environments across various industries, including aerospace, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
