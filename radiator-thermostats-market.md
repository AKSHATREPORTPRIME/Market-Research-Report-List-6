<p><p>Radiator thermostats come in two main types: angled and straight. Angled thermostats are designed for installations where the radiator pipe enters at an angle, allowing for efficient placement and better space utilization. In contrast, straight thermostats are suitable for radiators with direct, vertical pipe connections. Both types enable precise temperature control, enhancing energy efficiency and comfort in indoor spaces. Their choice depends on the specific piping configuration and aesthetic preferences of the room.</p></p>
