<p><p>The Short Peripheral Intravenous Catheter (PIVC) market is categorized into two main types: ported and non-ported. Ported short PIVCs have a built-in access port that allows for multiple infusions without needing to reposition the catheter, enhancing convenience and efficiency in patient care. Non-ported short PIVCs, on the other hand, do not feature an access port and are typically used for single infusion purposes. Both types play crucial roles in intravenous therapy, catering to different clinical needs and preferences in healthcare settings.</p></p>
