<p><ul><li>Ultrasound Bone Densitometer</li><li>X-ray Bone Densitometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bone densitometry equipment is vital for diagnosing and monitoring bone health, particularly for osteoporosis. There are two primary types: ultrasound bone densitometers and X-ray bone densitometers. </p><p>Ultrasound bone densitometers use sound waves to assess bone density, offering portability and no radiation exposure, making them suitable for preliminary screenings. In contrast, X-ray bone densitometers, commonly known as dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (DXA) scanners, provide precise measurements of bone mineral density using low-dose X-rays, making them the gold standard in clinical settings for detailed evaluations.</p></p>
