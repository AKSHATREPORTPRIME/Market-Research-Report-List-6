<p><p>The high-performance inertial sensors market is primarily divided into two main types: high-performance angular rate gyros and high-performance linear accelerometers. High-performance angular rate gyros measure rotational velocity, enabling precise navigation and stability control in applications such as aerospace and automotive systems. High-performance linear accelerometers, on the other hand, measure linear acceleration, providing critical data for motion detection and positioning. Both sensor types are essential in delivering enhanced accuracy, reliability, and performance in various high-tech industries, including defense and consumer electronics.</p></p>
